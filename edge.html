How-to for Microsoft Edge

If you are using a different browser, click here.


Use the buttons below to speed up or slow down videos on YouTube, Netflix, Vimeo, and many other video platforms.
Note that the buttons only work with HTML5 video players.

Before installing the buttons, make sure that the Favorites Bar is visible. To do so, click on the three dots icon, go into Settings > Favorites bar, and turn on Show the favorites bar.

To correctly install a button, follow these steps:

  1. In the address bar, click on the star icon to perform the Add to favorites or reading list action. In the panel that appears, click on Favorites (under Save in) to expand the folders view.
  2. Click on the Favorites folder to show the Favorites Bar folder. Select the Favorites Bar folder and click the Add button. A new bookmark should appear on the favorites bar.
  3. Now click on the button you wish to install (e.g., 2x). A message should appear, click Ok to close it and proceed to the next step.
  4. Right click on the previously created bookmark and select Edit URL. Delete all the text in the panel that appears, then right click in the panel and select Paste. Finally, press Enter on your keyboard to close the panel. The button should now be installed and functional.
  5. Optionally, rename the bookmark by right clicking on it and selecting Rename.

When watching a video, click the desired button to change the playback speed.
Clicking a button again will reset the playback speed to the default one.
